About ArenaSync

We are ArenaSync — a gaming event platform built by gamers, for gamers. Our mission is to sync communities through live tournaments, esports showcases, and shared gaming experiences that unite players across all genres and platforms.

Founded in 2025 as a student-led initiative at Griffith College Cork, ArenaSync was born from a simple idea: gaming is better together. From local LAN events to global online tournaments, we build the infrastructure that makes it happen.

Our platform covers everything from upcoming event listings and organiser tools to ticketing and live RSVP management. Whether you're a casual player looking for your next event or a competitive organiser building a tournament circuit, ArenaSync has you covered.

Every arena needs a sync. Welcome to yours.
